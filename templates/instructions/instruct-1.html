Instructions

To begin, place your fingers on the "Y" and "N" keys and LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys

When you begin the task, you will see a black dot in the center of the screen. At the top is a scoreboard indicated as "Score: " in white, and a multiplier indicated as "MP: " in green.

When you answer correctly, your score will be shown on the scoreboard. If you answer correcly 3, 4, or 5 times in a row, your score will be multiplied by the number of times in a row you answered correctly (e.g. answer correctly 3 times in a row = score multipled by 3).
